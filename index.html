Kidney Cancer

Advanced surgical treatments for kidney cancer with minimally invasive techniques for faster recovery and better outcomes.

Common treatments include:
  • Laparoscopic Radical Nephrectomy
  • Laparoscopic Partial Nephrectomy

Treatment Details

Laparoscopic Radical Nephrectomy

A minimally invasive procedure to remove the entire kidney affected by cancer.

  • Smaller incisions compared to traditional surgery
  • Shorter hospital stay and recovery time
  • Less post-operative pain and complications
Laparoscopic Partial Nephrectomy

A kidney-sparing surgery that removes only the tumor while preserving healthy kidney tissue.

  • Preserves kidney function
  • Reduces risk of chronic kidney disease
  • Ideal for smaller tumors
Why Choose India for Kidney Cancer Treatment?

Our partner hospitals feature state-of-the-art urology departments with experienced surgeons who have performed thousands of successful kidney procedures at a fraction of the cost compared to Western countries.
